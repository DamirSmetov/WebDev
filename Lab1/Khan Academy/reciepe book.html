Recipe: Pizza

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Yeast 1 tablespoon
Water 3/4 cup
Olive oil 2 tablespoons
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Cheese 1 cup
Toppings (pepperoni, vegetables, etc.) As desired

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 220°C (430°F).

Step 2: Mix flour, yeast, water, and olive oil to form a dough.

Step 3: Roll out the dough and spread tomato sauce evenly.

Step 4: Add cheese and your favorite toppings.

Step 5: Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden brown.

Recipe: Burger

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Ground beef 200 grams
Salt and pepper To taste
Burger buns 2
Cheese slices 2
Lettuce 2 leaves
Tomato slices 4
Onion slices 2
Condiments (ketchup, mustard, etc.) As desired

Step 1: Season the ground beef with salt and pepper and form into two patties.

Step 2: Cook the patties in a skillet over medium heat for about 3-4 minutes per side.

Step 3: Place a slice of cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.

Step 4: Toast the burger buns if desired.

Step 5: Assemble the burger with lettuce, tomato, onion, patties, and condiments.
